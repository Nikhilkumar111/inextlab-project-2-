Company Name: iNextLabs
Founded: 2020
Headquarters: Singapore
Office Locations: India, Malaysia, United States

Company Overview:
iNextLabs is a Generative AI-focused company that builds intelligent AI agents on the inFlow platform. These agents can think, plan, and act to automate enterprise workflows.

Platform:
inFlow Platform – used to build intelligent AI agents.

Main Products:
• inFlow DocsAI – automates document-heavy workflows by extracting, summarizing, and classifying data.
• inFlow InsightsAI – analyzes business data, predicts trends, and provides actionable recommendations.
• inFlow EngageAI – provides multilingual AI agents for WhatsApp, web, and voice.

Mission:
To empower businesses to harness AI to redefine customer experiences and optimize operations.
